# Chaos Lab (TEU)

Welcome to Chaos Lab (TEU).

Our research focuses on AI-native networking, task-oriented network services (TONaaS), and autonomous network systems (AutoNaaS).

## Research Areas

- Task-Oriented Network as a Service (TONaaS)
- Autonomous Network as a Service (AutoNaaS)
- Edge Intelligence and IoT Systems
- Resource Orchestration in AI-native Networks
- Disaster-resilient Communication Systems

## Mission

We aim to build intelligent, adaptive, and task-driven network infrastructures for next-generation communication systems.
